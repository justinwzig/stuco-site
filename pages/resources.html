Grant Proposals

Guidelines:

  • A group or individual initiative must be evident prior to the proposal to Student Council. (e.g. fundraising in and out of school, using own money, etc.).
  • The actions taken with the aid of the granted money should benefit the school, students, or school environment in some way.
  • No loose ends or variables should be present; the specifics of the proposal need to be set (e.g. dates, number of people involved, transportation if necessary, etc.).
  • A specific amount of money must be requested. No cost ranges, guesses, or asking for "as much as Student Council will give."
  • The presentation should be concise, clear, and around 5 minutes. (PowerPoints and hard copies of the proposal are suggested.)

Necessary Information:

  • Grant Form
    • Name of Group/Student
    • Teacher Sponsor or affiliated groups
    • Name of Project
    • Briefly explain the project
    • Reasons you want to complete the project
    • Who will be affected by the project
    • Timeline of the project
    • Suggested amount you are requesting
